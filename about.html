About Us

About Us.

Hilley Consultants Limited is a medium-sized Mechanical, Consulting and Supplies company that was incorporated on 6th July 2015 with the Registrar of Companies (certificate of incorporation no: CPR/2015/197412) Kenya in reference to pursuant and in accordance with provision of the registration of companies Act and rules made there under (Cap. 486).

Hilley Consultants Limited is an equal opportunity employer which recognizes the importance of protecting and preserving environment, caring for the health and safety of our clients property and staff based on sound CSR practices and principles. Our success is based on the continuous commitment that we gives to all our clients by continuously updating and developing our services, and importantly, in this time of rising costs competitive pricing structure. Our consistency of work quality, experienced manpower, fast reliable service, and preventative maintenance have also contributed towards the success to date of Hilley Consultants Limited.

Mission
To build long term relationships with our customers and clients and provide exceptional customer services by pursuing business through innovation and advanced technology.
Vision
To provide quality services that exceeds the expectations of our esteemed customers.
Purpose
To be a leader in building and mechanical works, refrigeration, Air conditioning and mechanical ventilation by providing enhanced services, relationship and profitability.

Goals

      ✔ Regional expansion in the field of refrigeration, Air conditioning and mechanical ventilation and develop a strong base of key customers.

      ✔ Increase the assets and investments of the company to support the development of services.

      ✔ To build good reputation in the field of Refrigeration, Air conditioning and mechanical ventilation and become a key player in the industry.

Core Values

      ✔ We believe in treating our customers with respect and faith.

      ✔ We grow through creativity, invention and innovation.

      ✔ We integrate honesty, integrity and business ethics into all aspects of our business functioning.
